The current favorites

Colton Hyer
Oct 18, 2023

As the 2023 champions league tournament kicked off just over a month ago, now is the perfect time to take a look at likely favorites, as well potential cinderlla stories. To understand the current favorites, its first important to look at who did well in the competition last year. The winners of the 2022 champions league were Manchester City. Under their manager Pep Guardiola, City have continued to dominate heading into the 2023 season and have their eyes set on back to back trophies. Other strong contenders who performed well last season include Real Madrid, Bayern Munich, and Paris Saint-Germain (PSG).
